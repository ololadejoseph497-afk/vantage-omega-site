# Privacy & Fee Disclosure Policy

**Effective Date: March 2026**

This document outlines the operational transparency and data handling practices for the VantagePro Ω Terminal.

### 1. Data Minimization
VantagePro Ω does not collect, store, or sell personal user data. All configuration settings and API credentials remain on the user's local device.

### 2. Fee Structure & Settlement
The terminal utilizes the **Paystack Transfer API** to facilitate the automated collection of a **2% Protocol Fee** from net trading profits. 
* Commissions are calculated per profitable trade.
* Payouts are settled to the developer's registered Paystack account.
* All fees are clearly displayed in the "Enterprise Dashboard" for user audit.

### 3. Compliance
We operate in accordance with the 2026 Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) regarding local data encryption and user-initiated data erasure.
